Stanislaw-class Frontier Cruiser (CF-Z2S111) MCr 5130.8

Builder: Piorabanti
Owner: Baraccai Technum
Disposition: Detached Duty

Using a 4800-ton, TL20 hull, the Stanislaw Class Frontier Cruiser mounts jump drive-Z, maneuver drive-Z9, and power plant-Z9, giving a performance of jump-1 and 11G acceleration. Fuel tankage supports a 1 parsec jump, at 398t per parsec, and one month of operations. Attached to the bridge is a Computer Model/9 fib. There are 40 staterooms and no low berths. Installed weaponry include 1 Bay Particle Accelerator, 1 Bay CommCaster, 1 Bay Meson Gun, 1 Bay KK Missile, 1 Bay AM Missile, 1 Bay DataCaster, 1 Bay Stasis, 1 Bay Fusion Gun, 1 Bay Pulse Laser, 1 Bay Beam Laser, 1 Bay Sandcaster, 1 Bay Jump Damper, 1 Bay Tractor/Pressor, and 1 Bay Disruptor. Cargo capacity is 400 tons. The ship has a streamlined hull, with scoops, intakes, and bins for frontier refueling.

Carried craft include 5 Pinnace + Hangar spaces, and 5 Gig + Hangar spaces. The ship has 42 crew.

TL 20. Demand -5. Volume: 4670 tons. MCr 5130.8.
