Rrazaghz-class Corsair (P-DA42) MCr 153.1

Builder: Voukkoez
Owner: Vargr
Disposition: In Service

Built on a 400-ton TL-12 winged airframe, the Rrazaghz is a typical pirate ship, manned by vargr brigands, ranging the star lanes in search of vulnerable freighters and their cargoes.

It sports the Ruefaez Kava 2/D jump drive, the Voukkoez AM4 maneuver drive, and the Kuekhkhoe PL KK53 power plant, giving performance of Jump-2 and 4G acceleration. Fuel tankage supports four months of operations and one jump-2. On the bridge is a computer Model/5.

The keys to the corsair are its firepower and a powerful sensor suite. The Rrazaghz has four hardpoints, allocated to a particle accelerator bay, a missile launcher, a plasma gun, and a CommCaster. This class of ship is designed to work in packs to bring down larger targets.

There are 5 staterooms, a barracks for gunners and marines, and five emergency low berths. There are no ship's vehicles. Cargo capacity is 40 tons.

The Vargr corsair requires a crew of 7: pilot, astrogator, 2 engineers, medic, and 2 gunners. The ship can easily carry a crew of 27, which would include raiders or boarding party members. This ship does not normally carry passengers.

Crew Comfort -1. Demand -5. Volume: 400 tons. MCr 153.1.
