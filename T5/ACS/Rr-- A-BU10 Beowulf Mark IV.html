Beowulf Mark IV-class Trader (A-BU10) MCr 37.2

Owner: Republic of Regina

Using a 200-ton, TL18 hull, the Beowulf Mark IV Class Free Trader mounts Maneuver drive-A, Hop drive-A, and Power Plant-A, giving a performance of one Hop-1 and 1G acceleration. Fuel tankage supports one month of operations and one Hop-1. Attached to the bridge is a Computer Model/1. There are 18 staterooms and 16 low berths. Cargo capacity is 75 tons. The ship has an unstreamlined hull, with scoops and intakes for frontier refueling.

The ship has 4 crew: pilot/astrogator, medic, steward, and steward/freightmaster. The ship's computer usually acts as Engineer. The ship can carry 14 passengers and 16 low passengers.

Volume: 196 tons. MCr 37.2.
