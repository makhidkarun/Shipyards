Arrghonz-class Battle Cruiser (CB-6VYS63) MCr 144223

Owner: Vargr

Using a 384,000-ton, TL14 hull, the Arrghonz Class Battle Cruiser mounts jump drive-Z240, maneuver drive-Z480, and power plant-Z480, giving a performance of jump-3 and 6G acceleration. Fuel tankage supports a 3 parsec jump, at 34560t per parsec, and 2 months of operations. Attached to the bridge is a Computer Model/6. There are 36 officer suites, 300 staterooms, 450 spacer niches, 450 spacer bunks, and no low berths. Gunnery barracks hold up to 1400 gunnery crew.

Installed weaponry include a Factor-T Meson Spine, 100 LBay Missiles, 100 LBay Particle Accelerators, 100 LBay Pulse Lasers, and 100 LBay Sandcasters. The ship has 6 layers of kinetic charged armor (AV 168), and mounts a meson screen, nuclear damper, and magnetic scrambler for defense. Cargo capacity is 1000 tons. The ship has a streamlined hull.

The ship carries 30 Fast Fighters (30 tons each). The ship has 2,670 crew, split into three shifts.

TL 14. Crew Comfort -3. Demand -5. Volume: 382330 tons. MCr 144223.
