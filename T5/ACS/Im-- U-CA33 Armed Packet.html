Armed Packet-class Packet (U-CA33) MCr 115.3

Owner: Imperial

Using an airframed 300-ton hull, the Iishillek-class Packet specializes in unscheduled passenger service for those in a hurry. It is equipped with the Madoc Mark 3 jump drive, the ISMM Model D.34 maneuver drive, and the Deltic Mk3323-D power plant, giving performance of Jump-3 and 3G acceleration. Adjacent to the bridge is a computer Model/3. As a formality, the ship comes with one missile launcher; the buyer is expected to purchase and install whatever weapons are needed. It has one ship's vehicle: an air/raft. The ship can fill its tank with pure fuel from gas giants (in 20 minutes), water sources (in about one hour), and ice (in about 90 minutes).

The ship requires a crew of 5: Pilot, Astrogator, two Engineers, and a Medic. There is an owner's suite, two staterooms, two spacer niches, and a medic's room with attached clinic. Up to three gunners and two additional personnel can bunk in the gunners' barracks. It can carry up to six high passengers, four middle, and four low. Cargo capacity is 4 tons. It has a one-ton vault for mail contracts.
