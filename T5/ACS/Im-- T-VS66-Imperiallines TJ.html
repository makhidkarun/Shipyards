Imperiallines TJ-class Transport (T-VS66) MCr 1099

Using a 2000-ton hull, the frontier transport is a cargo-carrying freighter employed to serve worlds off the main trade ruutes. It mounts jump drive-V3, maneuver drive S3, and power plant-S3, giving a performance of jump-6 and 6G acceleration. Fuel tankage for 1300 tons supports the power plant, and allows one jump-6. Adjacent to the bridge is a computer Model/6. There are 10 staterooms and ten low berths. The ship has 10 triple beam laser turrets. There is one ship's vehicle: a 45 ton shuttle. Cargo capacity is 194 tons (not including the shuttle's cargo bay). The hull is streamlined.

The frontier transport requires a crew of 10: captain/pilot, astrogator, medic, shuttle pilot, and 6 engineers. Gunners may be added as needed, with the spacer niches turning into hot bunk niches. The ship does not normally carry passengers.

Externally identical to the type TI frontier transpott, the type TJ is never openly identified as such by its owners. The type TJ fulfills Imperiallines' major covert mission: to provide a jump-6 transport system within the Imperium for material, agents, information, and equipment necessary for various Imperial activities.

TL 15. Crew Comfort -1. Demand -5. Overtonnage +28 tons. MCr 1099.
