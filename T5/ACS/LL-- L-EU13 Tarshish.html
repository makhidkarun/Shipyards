Tarshish-class Lab Ship (L-EU13) MCr 172.2

Owner: Llellewyloly

Using a 500-ton, TL16 hull, the Lab Ship is designed for wilderness research and transport operations. It mounts jump drive-H, maneuver drive-C, and power plant-C, giving a performance of jump-3 and 1G acceleration. Fuel tankage supports a 1 parsec jump, at 45t per parsec, and two months of operations. A Collector supports jump-3. Attached to the bridge is a Computer Model/3. There are 6 quad-capacity staterooms and 10 low berths. Research payload includes survey sensors, 10 tons of mission-specific space, 5 medical capsules, and one medical clone capsule. Cargo capacity is 50 tons. The ship has an unstreamlined hull.

The ship carries a Light Shuttle in a hangar. The ship requires 7 crew, and can carry 16 passengers and 10 low passengers.

Volume: 496 tons. MCr 172.2.
