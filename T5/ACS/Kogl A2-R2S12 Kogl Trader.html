Kogl Trader-class Far Trader (A2-R2S12) MCr 827.2

Using a 3000-ton, TL10 hull, the Kogl Far Trader mounts jump drive-Q2, maneuver drive-Q, and power plant-Q2, giving a performance of jump-2 and 1G acceleration. Fuel tankage supports a 2 parsec jump, at 300t per parsec, and 0.5 months of operations. Attached to the bridge is a Computer Model/3. There are 16 crew rooms, 8 passenger staterooms and 16 low berths. Installed weaponry include 2 T3 Missiles, 2 T3 Pulse Lasers, and 2 T3 Sandcasters. Cargo capacity is 200 tons. The ship has a streamlined hull, with scoops, intakes, and bins for frontier refueling.

The ship carries 1 Ship's Boat. The ship has 12 to 16 crew, and can carry 8 passengers and 16 low passengers.

All living spaces are ten times that of Human ships.

Crew Comfort +40. Demand +36. Volume: 3183.5 tons. MCr 827.2.
