Chiipsikaat-class Subsidized Liner (MK-AP01) MCr 14

Using a 100-ton, TL12 hull, the Chiipsikaat-class Subsidized Liner is mounts jump drive-A, maneuver drive-A, and power plant-A, giving a performance of jump-1 and 0G acceleration. Fuel tankage supports a 1 parsec jump, at 9t per parsec, and one month of operations. Attached to the bridge is a Computer Model/0 std. There are 10 staterooms and 14 low berths. Cargo capacity is 50 tons. The ship has a planetoid hull.

The ship has 4 crew, and can carry 10 passengers and 14 low passengers.

TL 12. Crew Comfort -3. Demand -1. Volume: 100 tons. MCr 14.
