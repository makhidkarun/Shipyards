Wildebeest-class Frontier Merchant (RF-JS33) MCr 259

Using a 900-ton, TL12 hull, the Wildebeest Class Frontier Merchant mounts jump drive-P, maneuver drive-P, and power plant-P, giving a performance of jump-3 and 3G acceleration. Fuel tankage supports a 3 parsec jump, at 90t per parsec, and one month of operations. Attached to the bridge is a Computer Model/3. There are 28 staterooms, two gunners' barracks, and 20 low berths. Installed weaponry include 3 T1 Beam Lasers, 3 T1 Sandcasters, and 3 T1 Missiles. Cargo capacity is 200 tons. The ship has a streamlined hull, with scoops, intakes, and bins for frontier refueling. Its undertonnage design gives it 4G acceleration in atmosphere 2+.

The ship carries one vehicle: a Slow Pinnace. The ship has 12 crew, and can carry 16 high passengers and 20 low passengers.

TL 12. Demand +1. Volume: 891 tons. MCr 259.
