Maada C-class Packet (U-BS44) MCr 74.6

Owner: Republic of Regina

Using a 200-ton, TL16 hull, the Maada Class Packet is designed to deliver and retrieve passengers to sensitive locations. It mounts D-class drives, giving a performance of jump-4 and 4G acceleration. Fuel tankage supports a 4 parsec jump, at 18t per parsec, and 1 month of operations. Attached to the bridge is a Computer Model/2. There are 4 crew staterooms, 4 passenger staterooms, and a clinic with 8 low berths. Installed weaponry include a T1 Missile, a T1 Beam Laser, and a T1 Sandcaster. A black globe is bolted in. Cargo capacity is 20 tons. The ship has a streamlined hull, with scoops for frontier refueling. It is capable of submerging into bodies of water for extended periods of time.

The ship has room for up to 4 crew. It can carry 4 high or medium passengers and 8 low passengers.

TL 16. Volume: 193.5 tons. MCr 74.6.
