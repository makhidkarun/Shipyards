Maranatha-class Yacht (Y-BU11) MCr 62.4

Builder: GsBAG
Owner: Judges Guild
Disposition: In Service

Using a 200-ton, TL12 hull, the MC&S Maranatha-class Yacht mounts advanced A-series drives, giving a performance of jump-1 and 1G acceleration. Fuel tankage supports a 2 parsec jump, at 16t per parsec, and one month of operations. Attached to the bridge is a Computer Model/1. There are four crew rooms, one owner-aboard suite, and ten passenger staterooms. There is one empty single turret installed. Cargo capacity is 13 tons. The hull is unstreamlined.

Carried craft include 1 Custom Fast Boat, 1 enclosed Air/Raft, and 1 ATV. The boat can carry the ATV. The ship has 4 crew (pilot/astrogator, engineer, medic, and steward), and can carry 11 high passengers.

TL 12. Demand +1. Overtonnage +7 tons. MCr 62.4.
