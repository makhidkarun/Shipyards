Maru-class Merchant (R-EU12) MCr 125.4

Using a 500-ton, TL13 hull, the Maru class Heavy Merchant mounts jump drive-E, maneuver drive-C, and power plant-E, giving a performance of jump-2 and 1G acceleration. Fuel tankage supports a 2 parsec jump, at 50t per parsec, and one month of operations. Attached to the bridge is a Computer Model/3. There are 16 staterooms and 14 low berths. Installed weaponry include 2 T3 Sandcasters and 3 T3 Pulse Lasers. Cargo capacity is 190 tons. The ship has an unstreamlined hull.

Carried craft include 1 Lifeboat. The ship requires 9 crew: captain/pilot, astrogator, two engineers, steward, medic, and three gunners. Additional gunners may be added. It can carry 7 high or medium passengers and 14 low passengers.

TL 13. Demand +2. Volume: 486.5 tons. MCr 125.4.
