Council Cruiser-class Cruiser (C-VS44) MCr 956.4

Owner: Zhodani

Commanded by a man named Vlezpridliashav, the Echtovr Dazhia is a 2,000 ton Zhodani Dodielchiatl-class Cruiser (aka Council Cruiser: a Cruiser owned by the Supreme Council (Qlomdlabr) of the Zhodani Consulate).

It is fitted with the Fliebr L-480T2 jump drive, the 5x47T2u Kadlitinj generator maneuver drive, and the Tobrjde T24+q power plant, giving performance of Jump-4 and 4G acceleration. Fuel tankage supports 4 weeks of operations and two jumps totalling up to 6 parsecs. On the bridge is a computer Model/5phot.

There are 17 staterooms, gunner barracks, and 8 emergency low berths. The ship has eight barbettes containing a mix of offensive and defensive emplacements. There are three ship's vehicles: one GCarrier in a hull niche, one 40-ton pinnace, and one Ninz-class Scout. Cargo capacity is 53 tons, plus 5 tons on the scout.

The Cruiser requires a crew of 7: pilot, astrogator, four engineers, and medic. It typically operates with a crew of 14, which adds a sensor tech, 4 gunners, and 2 scout crewmen. These also act as a security force, and are capable of operating the GCarrier and Pinnace. The ship can carry passengers in empty staterooms (up to 10 in the ship and 5 more in the scout).
