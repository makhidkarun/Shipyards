-class Defender (D-DA00) MCr 205

A Low Guard ship.

Using a 400-ton, TL13 hull, the Defender mounts G-drive K, giving an orbital performance of 5G for up to one year of operations. Attached to the bridge is a Computer Model/4. There are 27 staterooms and no low berths. Installed weaponry include 2 triple turret Beam Lasers, 1 triple turret Sandcaster, and 1 Bay Missile. Cargo capacity is 20 tons. The ship has an airframe hull.

Carried craft include 1 MAMBA-15 Attack Launch in a hull niche. The ship has 3 officers and 24 crew divided into three shifts.

Demand -5. Volume: 391 tons. MCr 205.
