Sundog-class Gunned Packet (UG-EA41) MCr 229.4

Type UG Gunned Packet (UG-EA42). Using a 500-ton, TL16 hull, the Sundog Class Gunned Packet mounts jump drive-C, maneuver drive-K, and power plant-K, giving a performance of jump-1 and 4G acceleration. It also is retrofitted with Hop-1 and Collector-1. Fuel tankage supports one jump-1 or hop-1, and one month of operations. Attached to the bridge is a Computer Model/4. There is 1 captain’s suite, 4 crew staterooms, a gunners’ barracks, a security squad barracks, 16 passenger staterooms, and 20 low berths. Installed weaponry include 1 T3 Missile, 1 T3 DataCaster, 1 T3 Sandcaster, and 2 T3 Beam Lasers. Cargo capacity is 50 tons. The ship has an airframe hull, with full frontier refueling capability.

The ship carries 1 Shuttle. The ship has up to 10 crew, and can carry 8 high passengers, 8 middle passengers, and 20 low passengers.

TL 16. Volume: 478 tons. MCr 229.4.
