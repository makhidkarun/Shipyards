Maada R-class Packet (U-BS42) MCr 95.1

Owner: Republic of Regina

Using a 200-ton, TL11 hull, the Maada-class Packet is designed to carry passengers in a hurry to get somewhere. It mounts jump drive-B, maneuver drive-D, and power plant-D, giving a performance of jump-2 and 4G acceleration. Fuel tankage supports a 2 parsec jump, at 20t per parsec, and one month of operations. Attached to the bridge is a Computer Model/1. There is a captain's suite, four crew staterooms, eight passenger staterooms, and eight low berths. Installed weaponry include a T2 Missile and a T2 Beam Laser. A black globe is bolted in. Cargo capacity is 25 tons. The ship has a streamlined hull, with scoops for frontier refueling. It is submersible in water. A vault stores important small items.

The ship has 5 crew, and can carry 8 high or medium passengers and 6 low passengers.
