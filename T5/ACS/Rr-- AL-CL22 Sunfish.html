Sunfish-class Exploratory Trader (AL-CL22) MCr 115.9

The Sunfish-class Frontier Trader is one way to make an impression. Its 300 ton hull fills a niche similar to the larger merchant cruisers: it is designed for chartered wilderness trade missions, in order to seek out potentially lucrative or key trade routes in wilderness areas. It sports the Madoc Mark 2 jump drive, the LSP Impulse #2 maneuver drive, and is capable of Jump-2 and 2G acceleration. It has 60 tons of fuel, enough for a 2 parsec jump at 27t per parsec, and one month of operations. It has a standard bridge with an adjacent Model/2bis. The ship has scoops, bins, and intakes for wilderness refueling. The hull is a lifting body, and capable of a sea landing.

There are 3 weapons installed standard: a dual sandcaster turret, a dual missile launcher, and a dual beam laser. There is a captain’s suite, four crew staterooms, and a gunners’ barracks with five bunks. The ship can carry 4 luxury passengers, 4 standard passengers, 8 low passengers, and has a 70 ton cargo hold.

There is one vehicle: a Fast Boat.

TL 15. Demand +1. Volume: 300 tons. MCr 115.9.
