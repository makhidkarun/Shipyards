Kogl Scout-class Scout/Courier (S-MA12) MCr 296.6

Using a 1200-ton, TL10 hull, the Kogl Scout/Courier mounts jump drive-M, maneuver drive-F, and power plant-M, giving a performance of jump-2 and 1G acceleration. Fuel tankage supports a 2 parsec jump, at 120t per parsec, and 0.5 months of operations. Attached to the bridge is a Computer Model/3. There are 12 staterooms and no low berths. Installed weaponry include 1 T1 Sandcaster, 2 T1 Missiles, and 2 T1 Pulse Lasers. Cargo capacity is 20 tons. The ship has an airframe hull, with scoops, intakes, and bins for frontier refueling.

The ship has 11 crew. All living spaces are ten times that of Human ships.

Crew Comfort +40. Demand -5. Volume: 1198.5 tons. MCr 296.6.
