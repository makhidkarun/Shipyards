Orca-class Transport (T-MA50) MCr 526.5

Builder: Clan Severn
Owner: Oberlindes

Using a 1200-ton, TL17 hull, the Orca Class Transport is equipped with the IDC FarDrive 1/F hop drive, the ISMM Model Z.51 maneuver drive, and the Zf-5 Cold Sun power plant, giving a performance of one Hop-1 and 5G acceleration. Fuel tankage supports one month of operations and one Hop-1. Attached to the bridge is a Photonic Model/6 computer. There is a captain's suite, three crew single staterooms, four crew double staterooms, eight passenger double rooms, and two passenger double suites. There are no low berths. Gunners' barracks bunks up to five.

Installed weaponry include two T3 Tractor/Pressors, and one B1 Fusion Gun. Defenses include integral charged anti-EMP armor and two Kurukashin Mark IV/L black globe generators. Cargo capacity is 500 tons. The ship has an airframe hull, with scoops, intakes, and bins for frontier refueling.

Carried craft include 4 Lifeboats in hull niches. The ship has 16 crew, and can carry 18 passengers.

Volume: 1198 tons. MCr 526.5.
