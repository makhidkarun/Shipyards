Using a 500-ton, TL17 hull, the BX7 Class Corvette mounts jump drive-H, maneuver drive-N, and power plant-Q, giving a performance of jump-3 and 5G acceleration. Installed weaponry include 1 T2 Beam Laser, 2 B1 Missiles, 1 T4 CommCaster, and 1 T2 DataCaster. Cargo capacity is 20 tons. The ship has a streamlined hull, with scoops, intakes, and bins for frontier refueling.

Carried craft include 1 Air/Raft Enclosed, and 1 Gig. The ship has 6 crew.
