Compact Marava-18-class Far Trader (A2-AU20) MCr 31.3

Using a 100-ton, TL18 hull, the Far Trader mounts maneuver drive-A, hop drive-A, and power plant-A, giving a performance of Hop-2 and 2G acceleration. Fuel tankage supports one month of operations and two Hop-2s. Attached to the bridge is a Computer Model/1 std. There are 12 staterooms and 8 low berths. Cargo capacity is 50 tons. The ship has an unstreamlined hull.

The ship has 3 crew, and can carry 8 passengers and 8 low passengers.
