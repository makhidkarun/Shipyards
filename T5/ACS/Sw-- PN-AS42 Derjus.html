Derjus-class Marauder (PN-AS42) MCr 54.5

Owner: Sword Worlds

A minimal raiding platform, the Derjus-class Light Marauder is equipped like a slow fighter, except with twin grapples each capable of carrying an additional 35 tons. Typically the ship will carry a 35 ton fuel pod, to extend the ship's range up to 4 parsecs. The design has also been known to carry a 4G, 35 ton MUQB utility boat for assaults.

TL 11. Demand -5. Volume: 65 tons. MCr 54.5.
