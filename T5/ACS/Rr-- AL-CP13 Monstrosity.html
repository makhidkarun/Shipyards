Monstrosity-class Exploratory Trader (AL-CP13) MCr 112.3

Owner: Republic of Regina

Using a 300-ton planetoid hull, the Monstrosity Class Exploratory Trader mounts jump drive-E, maneuver drive-B, and power plant-E, giving a performance of jump-3 and 1G acceleration. Fuel tankage supports a 1 parsec jump, at 27t per parsec, and one month of operations. Attached to the bridge is a Computer Model/3 . There are 2 staterooms and 10 low berths. Installed weaponry include 1 T3 Missile, 1 T3 Beam Laser, and 1 T3 Sandcaster. Cargo capacity is 40 tons, plus what the shuttle can carry.

Carried craft includes 1 grappled 70 ton shuttle. The ship has 10 crew, and can carry 8 passengers and 10 low passengers.

Overtonnage +31 tons. MCr 112.3.
