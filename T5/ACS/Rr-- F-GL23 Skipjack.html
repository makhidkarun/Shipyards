Skipjack-class Freighter (F-GL23) MCr 246.7

Owner: Republic of Regina

Using a 700-ton hull, the Skipjack-class Freighter is a large merchant starship designed to move cargo, and specializes in seaport-access. It is equipped with the IDC FarDrive 3/J jump drive, the 9x22Gk Ganged Forelle field generator maneuver drive, and the Torgee PL JJ36 power plant. It is capable of Jump-3 and 2G acceleration.

Fuel tankage supports a 3 parsec jump, at 70t per parsec, and one month of operations. Attached to the bridge is a Computer Model/3. There are 7 crew staterooms, 8 passenger staterooms, and no low berths. Cargo capacity is 247 tons. The ship has a lift body hull, with scoops, intakes, and bins for frontier refueling.

Carried craft include 2 enclosed Air/Rafts in hull niches. The ship has 7 crew, and can carry 8 passengers.

Crew Comfort -1. Overtonnage +2 tons. MCr 246.7.
