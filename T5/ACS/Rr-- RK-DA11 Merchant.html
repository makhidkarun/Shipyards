TL17-Merchant-class Subsidized Merchant (RK-DA11) MCr 110.1

Using a 400-ton, TL17 hull, the Subsidized Merchant mounts jump drive-B, hop drive-B, maneuver drive-B, and power plant-B, giving a performance of jump-1, hop-1, and 1G acceleration. Collectors support up to 2 jumps or 2 hops. Power plant fuel supports one month of operations. Attached to the bridge is a Computer Model/1 std. There are 27 staterooms and 10 low berths. Cargo capacity is 150 tons. The ship has an airframe hull, with scoops and intakes for frontier refueling.

The ship has 7 crew, and can carry 10 high passengers, 10 medium passengers, and 10 low passengers.

TL 17. Crew Comfort +1. Volume: 396.5 tons. MCr 110.1.
