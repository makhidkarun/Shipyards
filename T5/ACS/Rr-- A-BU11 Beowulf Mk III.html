Beowulf Mk III-class Trader (A-BU11) MCr 35.5

Owner: Republic of Regina

Using a 200-ton, TL17 hull, the Beowulf Mk III Class Trader mounts jump drive-A, maneuver drive-A, hop drive-A, and power plant-A, giving a performance of jump-1, hop-1, and 1G acceleration. Fuel tankage supports up to 2 jump-1s, at 20t per parsec, and one month of operations. Attached to the bridge is a Computer Model/1 std. There are 11 staterooms, one owner's suite, and 6 low berths. Installed weaponry include 2 T1 turrets (empty). Cargo capacity is 60 tons. The ship has an unstreamlined hull, with scoops, intakes, and bins for frontier refueling.

The ship has 4 crew, and can carry 8 passengers and 6 low passengers.

TL 17. Crew Comfort +1. Volume: 199.5 tons. MCr 35.5.
