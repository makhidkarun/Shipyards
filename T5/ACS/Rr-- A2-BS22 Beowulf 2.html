Beowulf 2-class Far Trader (A2-BS22) MCr 59.1

Owner: Republic of Regina

Using a 200-ton, TL15 hull, the Beowulf/2 class Far Trader mounts 'B'-class drives, giving a performance of jump-2 and 2G acceleration. Fuel tankage supports a 2 parsec jump, at 18t per parsec, and 2 months of operations. Attached to the bridge is a Computer Model/2. There is a captain's suite with a mail vault, 4 crew staterooms, 8 passenger staterooms and 8 low berths. Installed weaponry include a T2 Missile and a T2 Beam Laser. Cargo capacity is 50 tons. The ship has a streamlined hull, with scoops, intakes, and bins for frontier refueling.

The ship carries an enclosed Air/Raft in a hull niche. It requires 5 crew, and can carry 8 passengers and 8 low passengers. It has fittings to carry a bolted-in black globe generator, if desired.

TL 15. Overtonnage +4 tons. MCr 59.1.
