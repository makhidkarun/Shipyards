Orizae-class Corsair (P-DA42) MCr 145.2

Owner: Vargr

Using a custom 400 ton Airframe hull, the Orizae is a corsair design manufactured in the Tuglikki sector and sold to traders who travel in unpatrolled regions, or who travel without gunned escorts.

It is fitted with the Ghafo L-226D jump drive, the Vaeno Gravitics H4.4 maneuver drive, and the Gozoukhs Power H power plant, giving performance of Jump-2 and 4G acceleration.
