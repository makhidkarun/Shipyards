Kforgzue-class Privateer (PQ-DA42) MCr 157.4

Owner: Valtra

Using a 400-ton, TL16 hull, the Kforgzue Class Privateer is fitted with the Ghafo L-226D jump drive D, the Vaeno Gravitics H4.4 maneuver drive H, and the Gozoukhs Power power plant H, giving a performance of jump-2 and 4G acceleration. Fuel tankage supports a 2 parsec jump, at 33t per parsec, and one month of operations. Attached to the bridge is a Computer Model/2 std. There is one owner's suite, one XO stateroom, 8 standard staterooms and 12 low berths. Installed weaponry include 2 T3 Missile, and 2 T3 Beam Laser. Two barracks bunk up to 10, including gunners, troops, and additional personnel. The ship has an airframe hull, with scoops and intakes for frontier refueling.

The main cargo hold displaces 72 tons. Advanced drive systems create a 16 ton auxiliary hold in the fuel spaces, and a 40 ton auxiliary hold in the engine room. There is one hangar, rated for a 40-ton ship.

The ship has 10 crew: captain, XO, pilot, astrogator, sensop, medic, and four gunners. The gunners typically bunk in the barracks. The ship can carry up to 32 people: 10 in staterooms, 10 in barracks, and 12 in low berths.

Demand -5. Volume: 398.5 tons. MCr 157.4.
