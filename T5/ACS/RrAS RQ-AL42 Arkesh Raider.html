Scout-Raider-class Raider (RQ-AL42) MCr 67.2

Owner: Arkesh Spacers

Using a 100-ton, modified scout/courier hull, the Scout-Raider mounts jump drive-A, maneuver drive-B, and power plant-B, giving a performance of jump-2 and 4G acceleration. Fuel tankage supports a 2 parsec jump, at 10t per parsec, and 2 months of operations. Attached to the bridge is a Computer Model/2 std. There are 4 double staterooms and no low berths. Installed weaponry include one short-ranged Bay KK Missile. Cargo capacity is 3 tons. The ship has a lift body hull, with scoops for frontier refueling. It is overtonned, meaning it lacks atmospheric agility and may have somewhat sluggish controls overall.

The ship has 6 crew.

Crew Comfort -3. Demand -5. Overtonnage +10 tons. MCr 67.2.
