Grendel-class Far Trader (A2-DA11) MCr 109.7

Owner: Republic of Regina

Using a 400-ton, TL17 hull, the Grendel Class Far Trader mounts jump drive-B, hop drive-B, maneuver drive-B, and power plant-B, giving a performance of jump-1, hop-1, and 1G acceleration. Fuel tankage supports a 2 parsec jump, at 40t per parsec, and one month of operations. Attached to the bridge is a Computer Model/1 std. There is a captain's suite, 6 crew staterooms, a gunners' barracks, 8 double-occupancy passenger suites, and 8 low berths. Installed weaponry include 1 T3 Beam Laser, 1 T3 Sandcaster, and 2 T3 Missiles. Cargo capacity is 90 tons. The ship has an airframe hull, with scoops, intakes, and bins for frontier refueling.

The ship carries 1 Launch. The ship has 7 crew (not including gunners), and can carry 16 passengers and 8 low passengers.

TL 17. Volume: 397 tons. MCr 109.7.
