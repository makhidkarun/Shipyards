Sunfish-class Frontier Merchant (RF-ES42) MCr 201.9

Builder: DyStar Madoc
Owner: Republic of Regina

Using a 500-ton, TL16 hull, the Sunfish Class Frontier Merchant fills a niche similar to the larger merchant cruisers: it is designed for exploratory trade missions, in order to seek out potentially lucrative or key trade routes in wilderness areas.

It mounts jump drive-E, maneuver drive-K, and power plant-K, giving a performance of jump-2 and 4G acceleration. Fuel tankage supports a 2 parsec jump, at 50t per parsec, and one month of operations. Attached to the bridge is a Computer Model/3. There are 26 staterooms and 14 low berths. Installed weaponry include 1 T2 Sandcaster, 2 T2 Missiles, and 1 T2 Beam Laser. Cargo capacity is 70 tons. The ship has a streamlined hull, with scoops, intakes, and bins for frontier refueling.

The ship carries 1 Fast Boat in its own hangar. The ship has 11 crew plus a gunners' barracks. It can carry 8 high passengers, 8 medium passengers, and 14 low passengers.

TL 16. Volume: 496 tons. MCr 201.9.
